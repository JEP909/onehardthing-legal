ONE HARD THING

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 21, 2025

One Hard Thing is operated by Jack Price ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Email address, username, and optional display name.

User Content: Photos you submit to daily challenges and optional reflection text.

Usage Data: Voting activity, Elo rating history, streak data, and app interactions.

Device Information: Device type, operating system, timezone, and push notification tokens (if enabled).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App
  • To calculate and display Elo ratings and leaderboards
  • To send push notifications about daily challenges and results (if enabled)
  • To display your photos in the community feed
  • To moderate content and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To improve our services

3. Information Visibility

Public: Your username, Elo rating, and submitted photos are visible to other users.

Private: Your email address, voting history, and blocked users list are not visible to other users.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers who help operate the App (Supabase, Expo)
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or the safety of users

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption via Supabase. Photos are stored in secure cloud storage. We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data is permanently removed.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the App
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications in Settings
  • Block other users
  • Report inappropriate content

To delete your account, go to Settings > Delete Account.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (authentication, database, and storage)
  • Expo (app framework and push notifications)

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of your data.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Elo history and leaderboard data may be retained for seasonal records. You can delete your account at any time to remove your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, please contact us at:
