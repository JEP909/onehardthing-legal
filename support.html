Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Profile tab, scroll down and tap "Delete Account". You'll be asked to confirm by typing DELETE. This action is permanent and will remove all your data.

When do new challenges appear?

New daily challenges are released every day at 9 AM EST. You have until 9 PM EST to submit your entry and vote on others.

How does the Elo system work?

When others vote "Resonate" on your submission, you gain Elo points. When they vote "Pass", you lose points. Your Elo rating reflects how well your submissions resonate with the community over time.

How do I enable or disable notifications?

Go to the Profile tab and toggle "Daily Prompt Reminders" on or off. We'll send you a notification when your daily challenge is ready and when your results are in.

I found inappropriate content. How do I report it?

Tap the flag icon on any submission in the feed to report it. You can also choose to block the user so you won't see their content again.

Can I change my username?

Yes, go to Profile and tap "Edit Profile". Note that you can only change your username once every 30 days.
